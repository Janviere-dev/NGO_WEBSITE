Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to empower and support rural youth

Isaro Program

The Isaro Program aims to educate and empower young girls in schools and youth centers on Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH). We establish clubs for safe learning and open discussions, ensuring that youth have accurate information and support on SRH.

These clubs meet regularly, offering interactive sessions, peer mentoring, and access to resources on SRH. Part of this program is supporting vulnerable girls with menstrual hygiene products to make sure that they do not miss school due to lack of pads.

Through the Isaro Program, we create safe spaces where young people can learn, ask questions, and receive the support they need to make informed decisions about their health and well-being.

Education Support Initiative (ESI)

The Education Support Initiative provides financial assistance to vulnerable girls who have dropped out of school due to financial constraints or family issues. By covering school fees and related expenses, this program helps these girls return to formal education, offering them a chance to continue their studies and pursue their dreams.

We work with their families to ensure that these girls have no other pressing challenges at home. Among these girls are young mothers who dropped out due to unwanted pregnancy, who are willing to go back to school.

Through ESI, we believe that education is a fundamental right and a powerful tool for transformation. We support each girl's journey back to school, providing not just financial assistance but also mentorship and ongoing support.

Teen Mothers Empowerment Program (TMEP)

The Teen Mothers Empowerment Program supports teen mothers who have experienced gender-based violence (GBV) or isolation from their families. We provide SRHR education, psychological support, and leadership skills to make informed decisions.

This program offers vocational training in marketable skills, enabling these young women to build a sustainable livelihood and regain their independence. We also provide support with basic needs, ensuring that these mothers and their children have access to essential resources.

Through TMEP, we help teen mothers rebuild their lives, develop confidence, and create better futures for themselves and their children. We believe that every young mother deserves the opportunity to thrive and succeed.

Empowering rural youth through education, health, and opportunity.
