Mission

Our mission is to support and empower rural youth, through sexual reproductive health, capacity building, and girls' education to improve their health and socioeconomic well-being.

Vision

We envision a society where rural youth can achieve their full potential in health, education, and socioeconomic well-being.

Our Programs

Isaro Program

Educating and empowering young girls in schools and youth centers on Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH), establishing safe learning spaces and peer mentoring.

Our Impact

We address the urgent need for sexual and reproductive health (SRH) education and support for girls in rural areas of Rwanda. We break the silence and provide comprehensive SRH education, focusing on empowering young girls and preventing challenges caused by lack of information.
